SUNDARBAN

-the mangrove forest

The sunderbans is one of the largest mangrove forest in the world.The forest lies at the confluence of ganges,bramaputro and meghna rivers in the bay of bangal. Heres the picture-

It becomes a UNESCO world heritaage site in 1997.

The Sunderban is famous for the royal bengal tiger as well as various species of birds, spotted deer, crocodile and snakes.

But now ,the sundarban forest is slowly getting destroyed by erosion,rising of sea-level,strom surges,and rising of salinity
